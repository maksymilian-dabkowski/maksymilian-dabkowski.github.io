Home

I am a lead research assistant at Prof. Scott AnderBois' linguistic laboratory at Brown University.

I work predominantly on A'ingae (or Cofán, ISO 639-3: con), an Amazonian language isolate spoken in northeast Ecuador and southern Colombia. My research interests include phonology and its interfaces, semantics, language documentation, language revitalization, logic, and psycholinguistics.
